Club Info

Our club's main goal is to learn and teach programming to students. People who don't know how to program will be able to learn from fellow students and once they do learn, or if they already know programming, will be able to then teach other students, participate in club and organized competitions and hackathons, as well as complete projects.

Club Advisor: Ms. Geisler

Usual Location: ISB Portable 3 (Ms. Geisler/Ms. Velasco's Room)

Usual Time: Second & fourth Monday of the month after school.
